## 🎯 Project Objectives

This project aims to:

- Compare the distribution of Movies vs TV Shows
- Identify the most common genres on Netflix
- Analyse global content production by country
- Examine how Netflix content additions have evolved over time
- Understand movie duration distribution patterns

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Movies significantly outnumber TV Shows in the dataset.
- Drama and Documentary-related genres dominate the platform.
- The United States produces the largest share of Netflix content.
- Content additions increased rapidly after 2015, peaking around 2019–2020.
- Most movies fall within the 90–119 minute duration range.
